Project Overview

Mobile User Experience of Mental Health Application
While TalkLife, an online peer to peer support network for mental health, has been systematically developed by professionals, this project aims to further assess the extent of accessibility and usability. Through qualitative and quantitative methods, this project provides insightful perspectives on the adjustment of particular app functionalities.

Impact of Interactive AI Technology on Product Perception
Part I: Virtual Assistant – Alexa, Siri, Google Home, Cortana
Part II: Emotional AI – VR System, Home Robot, Educational Robot, Brainwave Cat Ears
The findings reveal that product perception is shaped by social context, media form, marketing strategy and product cost.

Subreddit Analysis: How Does Dream of Traumatic Experience Shape Personal Narratives?
Based on the text data collected from Subreddits, this project suggests that particular content of dream may serve an essential role in shaping one's narrative, particularly that of traumatic experiences. This project sheds light on the potential of dream reconstruction in VR as an effective treatment technique.
