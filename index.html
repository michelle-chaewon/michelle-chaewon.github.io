Modeling Health Messages on Instagram
Collaborator: Hunter Priniski, Keith Holyoak
[Project in Progress] This research assesses and models health information posts on Instagram, and discusses how the resulting model can be leveraged to build a scalable intervention that reduces the mental health risks of some health influencers' posting behaviors.

Mobile UX Research on Mental Health Application
[Project in Progress] While TalkLife, an online peer to peer support network for mental health, has been systematically developed by professionals, this project uses both qualitative and quantitative methods to assess the extent of accessibility and usability of the app. Ideally, this study aims to create a prototype based on user feedback.

Subreddit Analysis: Retrieval of Traumatic Memories
[Project in Progress] Based on a large-scale text data collected from Subreddits (r/traumatoolbox, r/ptsd, etc.), this study reveals the potential factors that are correlated with the retrieval of truamatic memories. Ideally, this research will shed light on important elements that need to be carefully examined during my following study on content development of VR Exposure Therapy for PTSD.

Computational Tool for Conversational Analysis
Using Python and Elan, this project calculates complex occurences between eyebrow movement and particular word usage. The end goal is to introduce a computational method that efficiently processes conversational data. Code from this study will be used for my future project YouTube content analysis (e.g. TEDx Talks, interviews).

Impact of Interactive AI Technology on Product Perception
Part I: Virtual Assistant – Alexa, Siri, Google Home, Cortana
Part II: Emotional AI – VR System, Home Robot, Educational Robot, Brainwave Cat Ears
The findings reveal that product perception is shaped by social context, media form, marketing strategy and product cost.
