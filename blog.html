Awards & Achievements


Distinguished smile UP Ambassador
I was selected as a distinguished ambassador at smile UP (learning application). My primary duty was to 1. introduce and promote discussions on diverse topics such as technology and mental health, and 2. develop project ideas on communication technology that can help children without phones suffering from domestic violence during coronavirus (upcoming project).

Distinguished Brand Ambassador
I was selected as a distinguished brand ambassador at Samsung during the Paralympics. I was primarily responsible for introducing various functional benefits of Samsung VR and assisting international customers with disabilities to fully participate in VR experience.

2018 Kimbo Scholarship
I was awarded $2000 scholarship from Kimbo Foundation. According to the website, the mission of the Kimbo Foundation is to recognize and encourage students who show exceptional academic achievement and leadership potential for the Korean community, and to provide them with financial support in their endeavour to study.
